VPN Setup & Usage (Proton VPN) 
So, why would we use a VPN to begin with? 
There are three main reasons as to why you should consider using a VPN and those three reasons being:                                                                                               1. Privacy: To keep what you're doing on the internet private, making sure your data is safe and out of harmsway                                                                                      2. Security: To protect personal data, especially on public networks                               3. Access: Somethings on the internet might be blocked or restricted and using a VPN can temporarily give you access to whatever it may be.
